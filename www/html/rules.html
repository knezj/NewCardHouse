Rules of the Game

Structure

Talk about how game structured

  • Played with four players
  • Each has 13 cards
  • Game is played in pairs
  • Players 1 and 4 vs 2 and 3

Overview

Talk about overview

  • Card ranked low-high
  • Low of 2 to the Ace
  • Dealer splits the cards
  • Each player must play suit drawn
  • Play another card if can't match
  • Highest card within a suit wins

Bid rules

A bid corresponds to the number of rounds a team thinks they can

    Rule 3
  • Bidding moves counter-clockwise
  • Player to the right of dealer bids
  • Start your bid at 7 up to all 13
  • Can't bid less than previous
  • Dealer has the right to match bid
  • Dealer precedence when tied

TARNEEB

Talk about TARNEEB

  • Winner of the bid chooses TARNEEB
  • TARNEEB is the primary suit
  • Any TARNEEB card can take the pot

Strategy

Talk about strategy

  • Play all 13 cards in the hand
  • Count number of rounds won
  • If rounds won exceeds the bid:
  • Team gets all the bid points
  • If rounds won is less than bid:
  • Team loses bid points from total

Outcomes

Talk about outcomes

  • You collected the bid points per round
  • If number of tricks exceeds bid:
  • You win the round
  • The dealer plays the first card
  • Winner of each trick starts
  • Team that gets 42 points wins!
